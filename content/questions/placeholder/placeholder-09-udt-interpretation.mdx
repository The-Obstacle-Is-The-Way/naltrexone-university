## Stem

Which statement about urine drug testing is most accurate?

## Explanation

Screening immunoassays can cross-react and produce false positives or false negatives; confirmatory testing (e.g., GC/MS or LC-MS/MS) is used when results are unexpected or have high-stakes implications.
