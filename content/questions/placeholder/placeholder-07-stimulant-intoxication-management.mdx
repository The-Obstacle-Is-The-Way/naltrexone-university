## Stem

A patient presents with severe agitation, hypertension, and tachycardia after using cocaine. What is the most appropriate initial management?

## Explanation

Initial management of stimulant intoxication focuses on supportive care and symptom control; benzodiazepines are commonly used for agitation and autonomic hyperactivity.
